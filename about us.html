about us

Build A Story was a concept I came up with as a creative writer myself who wanted to create a platform to share my writing and collaborate with other budding writers.

One of the biggest things about build a story is the collaborative element. You are able to write part of a story - say the first 300 words - and leave it up to the rest of the build a story community to complete it, and watch your short story transform into something really special.



WHAT WE DO


write your own stories

write your own stories, whether its a classic romantic, or a chilling horror - let the community leave their feedback and find a safe space to read and write.

collaborate on other writers' stories

build a story gives you the opportunity to work with other writers to start, continue, or end a collaborative story. You can make new friends, learn from each other, and write fantastic stories together.

challenge yourself

Complete daily story writing challenges to test your writing skills and get out of your comfort zone.
